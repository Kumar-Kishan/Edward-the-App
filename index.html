Hey, you.

Wanna write a book?

Sidebar is a writing app designed for authors. It supports (and encourages) outlining, note-taking, rapid iteration, and easy context-switching.

Why Sidebar?

You've probably written documents in a word processor (like Microsoft Word). Word processors are great at helping you make your document look professional. But they look like this:

It's...blank. No place for your outline. No place for your research. No place for inspiration. If this is where you write, you'll spend a lot of time staring at a blinking cursor.

Sidebar is different. It's an app for the writing process: research, outline, development, and analysis. The only thing it doesn't do is advanced formatting - but that's what your word processor is for.

Sidebar looks like this:

Is that better or what? On the left is a simple word processor, with a row of tabs above it so you can switch between chapters with a single click. Next to it is a zoomed-out view of your chapter, with a built-in magnifier so you can scan and search your chapter instantly. And on the right is your outline, containing notes, plans, citations, and anything else you want to keep handy while you write.

By the way, Sidebar isn't just for books. It's great for essays, speeches, and scripts too.

Sidebar is still under construction. All your work is saved in the browser, but you shouldn't rely on it. Upcoming features include:

  • Auto-save your work to the cloud and work on it from any computer, anywhere.
  • Choose from a set of prefabricated outline templates to help you get started right, regardless of the project.
  • Export your entire book to a Microsoft Word document (.docx).
  • Several analyses to help you understand and improve your work.
  • Tools to help you overcome writer's block.
  • Contextual writing tools like an on-demand dictionary/thesaurus.
  • Support Sidebar with a $1/month premium account.
  • And more!
