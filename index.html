Edward is an app for new authors. He makes it easier to write a novel.

Unlike word processors, Edward is designed for every stage of writing: world-building, character creation, brainstorming, plotting, writing and rewriting. And he's full of useful features.

  • Your work is saved automatically every time you make a change. Focus on your writing and the app will take care of the rest.
  • Outline templates give you a running start. Choose the "Novel" template and Edward will lay out all the ingredients you need to bring your story to life.
  • Get an instant word, paragraph, and minute count for the current chapter and document at any time. Whether your goal is 80,000 words or 300 minutes, it's easy to keep an eye on your progress and stay motivated.
  • Use the interactive mini-map to effortlessly scan, search and jump from paragraph to paragraph in your novel. It's the ultimate bird's-eye view of what you've written so far.
  • Search instantly by word or phrase. If you forget a character's name or misplace a vital piece of research, finding it again only takes a few seconds.
  • Refer to the on-demand dictionary/thesaurus to find the perfect word for any situation, or to make sure that "inconceivable" means what you think it means.
  • Get notes or chapters out of the way with the fully restorable archive, then bring them back whenever you're ready. It's an easy way to remove distractions so you can stay focused on the next chapter or concept.
  • View data-driven charts and graphs about your novel to learn about your own style and find things to improve.
  • Download a PDF, Microsoft Word document, or full backup of your novel at any time. As soon as you're ready to publish, all you have to do is click a button.
  • Your Limited account is free forever. Try out the app for as long as you want (no credit card required) and upgrade when you're ready.
  • Upgrade to a Premium account to upload all your work to the cloud. We'll back up it up regularly so it never gets lost, and you can work on it from any computer, anywhere.
