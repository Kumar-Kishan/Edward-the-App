Edward is an app for new authors. He makes it easier to write a novel.

Here's some of what he has to offer:

Writing made simple with chapter tabs, a mini-map, a dictionary and thesaurus, side-by-side notes, and more.
Pre-made outline templates to get you started faster.
Visual analyses to help you understand your work.
Outlines to help you sketch out the most important parts of each chapter.
Plans to help you keep track of unlimited ideas, notes, and research.

If you're discouraged by blank pages or stumped by writer's block, Edward can help. He's an app for the writing process: planning, outlining, writing, and editing.

Edward has everything you need:

  • All your work is saved automatically, every time you make a change.
  • Outline templates for novels, essays, and scripts, so you can get started faster.
  • Instant word, paragraph, and minute count for the current chapter and document.
  • Fully interactive mini-map.
  • Instant search by word or phrase.
  • On-demand dictionary/thesaurus.
  • A permanent archive: keep everything you write without cluttering your workspace.
  • Data-driven charts and graphs about your novel.
  • Backups and PDF downloads of your work at any time.
  • With a Premium account, your work is saved in the cloud so you can work on it from any computer, anywhere. (Premium accounts are $2.46 per month.)
