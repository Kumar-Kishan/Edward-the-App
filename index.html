Wanna write a book?

Sidebar is a writing app designed for authors. It supports (and encourages) outlining, note-taking, rapid iteration, and easy context-switching.

Why Sidebar?

You've probably written documents in a word processor (like Microsoft Word). Word processors are great at helping you make your document look professional. But they look like this:

It's...blank. No place for your outline. No place for your research. No place for inspiration. If this is where you write, you'll spend a lot of time staring at a blinking cursor.

Sidebar is different. It's an app for the writing process: research, outline, development, and analysis. The only thing it doesn't do is advanced formatting - but that's what your word processor is for.
